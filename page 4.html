ABOUT GLARSES

Glarses made his first video on February 10 2020 and is a tech youtuber that specializes in keyboards and other keyboard related items or products.
Glarses or Dave is 24 years old born on November 25 1999.
and lives in the UK, he has had many big and well known collaborators on his channel like KSI Tommy Innit and Tubbo.
Glarses is known for his absurd keyboard ideas and challenges he takes on, Due to his quick thinking and crazy video ideas Dave
was able to become one of the top keyboard related content creators on the platform.
He was so popular to the point one of the largest switch making companies CHERRY MX did a collaboration
with him in making his greatest accomplishment the Cherry MX Purple. Another notable thing Dave has done is the creation of his very own company called "The Glarses Company"
Sadly Glarses hasn't revealed much about his private or personal life so all the information above is all we know about him
